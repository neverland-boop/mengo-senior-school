About Mengo

Grooming minds with competence and excellence since 1895.

Our Mission

To be at the Helm of Producing Competent Human Resources to serve the Church, State and the World

Our Vision

To Provide Quality Education through Practical skills, Teamwork, Self-reliance and God fearing persons

Core Values

Fearing God, Respect of Person & Property, Integrity

Our History

1895 — Founded

Established by the Church Missionary Society on Namirembe Hill as one of East Africa's first formal schools in a small house of a British woman.

2015 — Centenary

Celebrated 120 years with the inauguration of the Centenary block which cubbed the increasing pressure from numbers.

2026 — Expansion

Mega Complex Hall has been started to accomodate various activities int the school.

Today

Over 8600 students across O- and A-Level, with a DIT school producing students who can work their minds out
